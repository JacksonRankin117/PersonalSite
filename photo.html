    Photography is my artistic medium of choice. I believe that a mind that is open to both seeing the world artistically and scientifically can create truly unique work. My approach combines technical skill with creative vision, allowing me to capture moments in a way that resonates with me.

    Totality

    This photo was taken during the total solar eclipse on April 8, 2024 in Bloomfield, IN, using a Sony a37 with an SVBONY 540mm f/7 scope. It was shot without a filter during totality.
