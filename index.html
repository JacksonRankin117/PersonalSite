    Portfolio Highlights

    Photography

    Old Trailer

    This photo was a spur of the moment thing. I was walking with my sister in her neighborhood, and I saw this old trailer out of the corner of my eye. I quickly got my Mamiya RB67 with a 50mm lens out and snapped the photo at 1/250" at f/22

    Totality

    This photo was taken during the total solar eclipse on April 8, 2024 in Bloomfield, IN, using a Sony a37 with an SVBONY 540mm f/7 scope. It was shot without a filter during totality.

    Uke

    This photo was taken in my family's home. I requested my dad to play the ukelele a little, and I set up the shot. This is one of the most meaningful photographs I have ever taken. Definitely one of my favorites.

    Programming

    Ray Tracing Engine - C#

    This was an incredible experience for me. I was always interested in graphics, so for a final project in an OOP course I made a ray tracing engine from a book called "Ray Tracing in One Weekend". It was challenging but rewarding to see the images come to life.

    Julia Set - Python

    This was a compounding project for me. I first made a program to make a Mandelbrot set, and then I made one to create a Julia Set. In the future I hope to make a program that animates many Julia sets together.

    Double Slit

    This program helped me to explore my interest of optics. I first have to define an aperture, and then I can simulate the diffraction pattern that results from it.

    Current Projects

    N-Body Sim

    This is a simulation of the N-Body problem, where multiple celestial bodies interact with each other through gravity. The simulation shows the orbits of the bodies over time.

    Julia Set Animation

    This is an animated Julia Set that I made using the Julia. It shows the evolution of the set over time, creating a mesmerizing visual effect. I am having weird outputs, so its not complete yet.

    Motivation

    My main source of motivation for my art and my programming is my curiosity. I want to capture raw moments in time, and explore the beauty of the world around me. I also want to learn more about the technical aspects of photography and programming, and how I can use them to express my creativity. For programming, I am particularly interested in the intersection of art and technology, and how I can use code to create visually stunning and thought-provoking experiences.
